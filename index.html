The Forest Of Us


Join The Forest

Hi! We hope you enjoy a walk or three through the forest.
The forest was built to showcase the magic inside people. To get to know how wonderful everyone else is. And to share stories.
Do you want to join the forest? We'd love to have you! Joining is easy!
All you have to do is take a video telling whatever part of your story you want to tell, in whatever way you way you want to tell it. Submit your one to two minute video here!
